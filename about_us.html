A botique studio specialising in Wedding Photography

Welcome to Auckland Photography. I'm a photographer and cinematographer who is excited to capture beautiful and precious moments with you. My name is Isuru Biyanwila, based in Auckland. For me, every occasion is something precious which you have put a great effort in to this moment which you have dreamed a long way. It would be my duty to capture those moments in an artistic manner that would allow you to travel back in memories and put a smile on your face.

I deeply enjoy my time taking natural and emotional moments which I utilise as a way of sharing my passion with the community. Every moments I capture would express it's own unique story and bring out the natural beauty. Through my lenses, I love to work with natural lights to bring the authenticity and to take the viewer to a journey of beautiful memories. This also would allow to bring the natural colours and breathtaking images to tell a real story.

Journey of seeing through a lense started at young age when I first picked up a camera. I started learning the skill by taking countless photos and spending my time with the camera. After seeing my captures, a friend of mine requested to capture one of his previous moments of life with the trust and belief in me. And it was the start of a big journey where now I thrive to push my boundaries and constantly experiment new ideas and techniques.

You could trust on my captures, as I aim to capture and generate photographs and cinematography that are emotionally bonding, filled with love and life.

Beyond my captures, I value my communication with you and to understand your requirements to tailor my service to go beyond your expectations and unique requirements. As you explore my portfolio, you would be presented with a picture of someone's precious moments and it would be my honour to capture those moments.

I thank you for visiting my site, and invite you to come along and witness a journey of my captures which resonate someone's most precious chapters of their life. I look towards to capturing your dream come right moments through my lenses to preserve for you and your loved ones, for generations to come.

Warmest regards,

Isuru Biyanwila.
