Welcome to Auckland Photography. I'm Isuru Biyanwila, a photographer and cinematographer based in Auckland. I am excited to capture beautiful and precious moments with you. For me, every occasion is precious, as I understand the great effort you have put into reaching this moment, which you have dreamt of for a long time. It is my duty to capture those moments in an artistic manner that allows you to revisit your memories and bring a smile to your face.

I deeply enjoy my time capturing natural and emotional moments, which I use as a way to share my passion with the community. Every moment I capture expresses its own unique story and brings out the natural beauty. Through my lenses, I love working with natural light to bring authenticity and take the viewer on a journey of beautiful memories. This also allows me to capture natural colors and create breathtaking images that tell a real story.

The journey of seeing through a lens started at a young age when I first picked up a camera. I began learning the skill by taking countless photos and spending a lot of time with the camera. After seeing my captures, a friend of mine requested that I capture one of his past life moments, placing trust and belief in me. This marked the beginning of a significant journey where I now thrive by pushing my boundaries and constantly experimenting with new ideas and techniques.

You can trust my captures, as I strive to create photographs and cinematography that are emotionally engaging, filled with love and life.

Beyond my captures, I value my communication with you and strive to understand your requirements in order to tailor my services to exceed your expectations and meet your unique needs. As you explore my portfolio, you will be presented with a collection of precious moments captured by someone, and it would be my honor to have the opportunity to capture those moments for you.

I thank you for visiting my site and invite you to join me on a journey through my captures, which resonate with the most precious chapters of someone's life. I look forward to capturing your dream-come-true moments through my lenses, preserving them for you and your loved ones for generations to come.

Warmest regards,

Isuru Biyanwila.
